What is MechieBro?

MechieBro is a hyper-local digital platform that connects vehicle owners with trusted nearby mechanics in real time. It combines map-first discovery, instant requests, transparent pricing, and UPI payments to make breakdowns simple and stress-free.

UrbanClap + Google Maps + UPI Built for India Map-first UX

The MechieBro Promise

  • Nearby verified mechanics with ratings
  • Instant booking or scheduled service
  • Live tracking and emergency response
  • Digital invoices and repair history

The Problem We Solve

For Customers

  • No trusted mechanic nearby
  • Overcharging and unclear pricing
  • No reviews or service history
  • Emergency breakdown anxiety

For Mechanics

  • No online visibility
  • Walk-in only business
  • Cash-only payments
  • No brand or ratings
